To bleed the brakes, start at the wheel farthest from the master cylinder. Have a helper press the brake pedal three times and hold it down. Open the bleeder valve a quarter turn; fluid and air will escape. Close the valve BEFORE the helper releases the pedal, or air gets sucked back in. Repeat until the fluid runs clear with no bubbles, topping up the reservoir every few cycles so it never runs dry. Then move to the next-farthest wheel and repeat the whole sequence.
